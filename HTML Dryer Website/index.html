Dryer Control

Drying systems for corn, grain, oil- seeds and rice.

The proper conservation of corn, grain, oilseeds and rice safe- guards their quality and their value for the producer. Together with cleaning and the storage method, drying plays a key role as well. Only if the moisture has been removed the raw products can be stored for a longer time and losses are avoided.

Applications

  • Collection Points: Drying of corn, grain and oilseeds to storage moisture level following cleaning upon delivery.
  • Terminals/handling facilities: Drying of corn, grain and oilseeds to storage moisture level to make the product suita ble for transportation.
